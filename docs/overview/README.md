## Quick Summary

Cordial Cantina (working title: Orbital Market Maker) is a concentrated liquidity market maker that applies physical modeling concepts from Riemannian geometry and classical mechanics to predictive market dynamics on Solana blockchain.

For detailed trading strategy, see [Strategy](../strategy/README.md).
